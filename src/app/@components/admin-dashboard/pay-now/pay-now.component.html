Track Your Application.

Welcome to Relevo Immigrations' application tracking service. If you've already submitted a visa
application or passport through Relevo Immigrations, you can track its status here. Please note
that this service provides information on the current location of your application/passport, but not
on the exact stage of the application process.


Once you've submitted your application/passport at Relevo Immigrations, you can track its status
using the methods below:


  • Online Tracking:
    Click here to enter your Tracking ID number, which you'll receive after your documents
    have been verified and you've had your appointment with us.

  • Chat Support:
    Connect with our support team through our help desk for real-time assistance and updates on your application.

  • Email Support:
    Reach out to our support team via email for any inquiries or updates regarding your application.

  • Helpline:
    For immediate assistance, you can contact our helpline at [phone to be provided].

  • In-Person Visit:
    Feel free to visit our office to track your application in person. Our staff will be happy to assist you.

  • SMS Updates:
    Opt for our SMS service to receive regular updates on the status of your application. Additional charges may apply for this service.

Once your application has been processed by the relevant visa office, you'll receive a decision
along with your passport. If a visa is granted, it will be affixed to your passport. If not, you'll receive
a letter explaining the decision.


We strive to provide a seamless and transparent application tracking experience for all our clients.
If you have any questions or concerns, don't hesitate to reach out to us.


Thank you for choosing Relevo Immigrations for your visa application needs. We look forward to
assisting you throughout the process.
